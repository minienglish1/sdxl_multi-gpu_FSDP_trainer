HARLEY QUINN AND HER GANG OF HARLEYS. Harley Quinn and Her Gang of Harleys #2 by Amanda Conner features a large, vividly colored face of Harley Quinn in the center, surrounded by smaller circular portraits of various characters. Harley is depicted in a dynamic pose, holding a large feather, and wearing her signature red and black outfit. The overall color palette is vibrant, with a mix of purples, reds, and blues.
