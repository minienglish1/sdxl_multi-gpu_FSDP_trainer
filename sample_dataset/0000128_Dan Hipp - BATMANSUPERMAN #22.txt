BATMANSUPERMAN #22 by Dan Hipp features two iconic superheroes, Batman and Superman, depicted in a playful and exaggerated manner. Batman, in the foreground, wears a heavily armored suit with various badges and emblems, including the 'BAT' logo on his chest. Superman, in the background, floats confidently with his iconic red cape and 'S' emblem on his chest. The background has a swirling pattern in shades of purple and blue.
